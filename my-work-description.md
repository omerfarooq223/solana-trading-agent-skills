# Work Description: Solana (SOL) Spot Crypto Trader

## Daily Tasks

- Monitor SOL price movements throughout the day
- Track 24-hour trading volume for SOL
- Analyze price charts to identify support and resistance levels
- Record all trades in a log for tracking purposes
- Calculate profits and losses from completed trades
- Check social media platforms (Twitter/X) for market sentiment
- Monitor news sites for Solana-specific updates and announcements
- Assess portfolio balance to determine appropriate position sizing
- Apply risk management principles to limit potential losses

## Decision Making Process

- Identify key support and resistance levels on price charts
- Determine entry points based on technical analysis
- Decide exit points using predetermined profit targets and stop-loss levels
- Calculate position sizes based on current portfolio balance
- Factor in market sentiment from social media and news sources
- Adjust trading strategy based on Solana ecosystem developments
- Evaluate risk/reward ratios before executing trades

## Current Pain Points

- Manual monitoring of price movements is time-intensive
- Constant vigilance required to catch favorable entry/exit points
- Tedious process of recording trades and calculating P&L manually
- Difficulty in consistently tracking market sentiment across multiple platforms
- Time-consuming position sizing calculations that need constant adjustment
- Risk of human error in manual record keeping and analysis
- Fatigue from repetitive tasks affecting decision quality
- Missing important updates due to information overload across various sources
